Hello ! So glad you stopped by !

I’m Kavya a freelance illustrator based in Bangalore. I create art around things that touch my heart and soul.My work is inspired by nature, nostalgia, relationships and emotions

I am a self taught illustrator . While I studied Computer science engineering I was introduced to graphic design which lead me exploring more about visual arts. I am always up for taking meaningful illustration courses to upgrade my skills . It all started 7 years ago as a hobby and not giving up on it lead to working as a freelance illustrator. Apart from freelance work I have also gained 5+ years of experience as a computer science lecturer and successfully taught web designing and programming to undergrads.

When I am not drawing, you can find me cooking, listening to my favourite music, watching  re-runs of Dexter’s Laboratory , Klaus , Up , In this corner of the world , Young Sheldon , BBT , Sherlock Holmes , Harry Potter , etc or you can see me with a canon camera clicking pictures of cute animals or candid shots of random people.
